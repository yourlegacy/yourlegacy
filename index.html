Wayne Simmonds

NHL Hockey Player & Philanthropist

Wayne Simmonds of the Philadelphia Flyers in the NHL is a player that exemplifies a perfect balance of professionalism and philanthropy. On the ice he explosive, hard hitting, ball of energy that has the grit and determination to battle with the toughest guys in the league.

Off the ice Wayne mentors and inspires today’s youth to follow their dreams and not allow any obstacles to get in the way of their passion. Wayne has founded a non-for-profit called Wayne’s Road Hockey Warriors that inspires young hockey players and raises funds for underprivileged youth to pursue their hockey passion.

A World Junior Hockey Championship Gold Medal and 5 NHL seasons under his belt, Wayne wants to share his wisdom and knowledge with the rest of the world.

Brandon Sinclair

Your Legacy Editor in Chief

Brandon Sinclair has been Editor-in-Chief since he founded Your Legacy in February 2013. His mission in life is giving people an opportunity to follow their passions and dreams. Growing up he was surrounded by many talented and successful people and is committed to helping ordinary people achieve extraordinary things.

In the past two years, while doing a lot of work in personal developmental technologies, Brandon has been working with and analyzing the daily life, habits, and routines of top athletes, musicians, celebrities, and entrepreneurs. He has been able to package it all together and deliver the exclusive content into the hands of ordinary people.
